About the Authors

Our Mission

This tool is designed to make looking up words fast, accessible, and informative. Users can find definitions, etymologies, pronunciations (IPA), examples, synonyms, and more — all in one place.

It's a clean, student-built dictionary site to help both native and non-native speakers better understand English vocabulary.
